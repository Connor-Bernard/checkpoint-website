What is Checkpoint

Checkpoint is a software that leverages Capture the Flage (CTF) style games to let students learn using the same platforms they would use in industry.

Student Focused

The platform focuses on ensuring students can make the most out of their college education using our platform.

Practical

This platform engages students with practical challenges that simulate the student's experience.

Accessable

Checkpoint leverage instructor-provided feedback to lead students down the correct path, ensuring they never get stuck.

Fully Customizable

Instructors can customize the challenges using an easy-to-use config file or our customizer command-line utility.

Research Driven

This project is backed with extensive research and user studies to prove just how impactful this teaching style is.

Analytics Engine

Our platform provides instructors detailed anaytics data to ensure assignments are accessible and clearly worded.
